# Code of Conduct

## Our Standards
- Be respectful and constructive.
- Assume good intent and focus on facts.
- Give feedback that is actionable and specific.

## Unacceptable Behavior
- Harassment, discrimination, or personal attacks
- Doxxing or sharing private information
- Disruptive or abusive communication

## Enforcement
Project maintainers may remove or edit content and may restrict participation
for behavior that violates this code.
